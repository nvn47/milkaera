About Us

Quality farmers products from farms near you.

Milka Era Private Limited., is based in Thrissur, Kerala. We are manufacturing and marketing Premium Organic Dairy products under the brand 'Milka Era'.

For generations, we are farmers, our main source of income comes from milk. Later we started other farming and agricultural by using organic compost obtained from dairy farming. We always give priority for purity and quality of milk. And demand for our organic milk keep on increasing, so as our farm.

We gather up many farmers in our locality and increase our production without compromising the quality. To make this beneficiary for farmers, we to extend our supply area to nearby districts.

  • 100% Organic milk products.
  • No chemicles added in our products.
